     <h2 class="text-3xl font-serif font-bold text-stone-900 mb-6">What do we do?</h2>
     <div class="space-y-6">
       <div>
          <img src="/images/campamento/CABA√ëAS.jpg" alt="Mesolithic Huts" class="w-full h-64 object-cover rounded-xl mb-4" />
          <h4 class="text-xl font-bold text-stone-800">Mesolithic Huts</h4>
          <p class="text-stone-600">We recreate models of circular Mesolithic huts made from cane from scratch, serving as the base for each group.</p>
       </div>    
       <div>
          <img src="/images/campamento/EXCAVACION.jpg" alt="Archaeological Excavation" class="w-full h-64 object-cover rounded-xl mb-4" />
          <h4 class="text-xl font-bold text-stone-800">Archaeological Excavation</h4>
          <p class="text-stone-600">Artificial excavation area with buried materials simulating different eras. We learn to dig, coordinate, draw findings, and restore.</p>
       </div>
       <div>
          <img src="/images/campamento/CARRERAS OBSTACULOS.jpeg" alt="Obstacle Courses" class="w-full h-64 object-cover rounded-xl mb-4" />
          <h4 class="text-xl font-bold text-stone-800">Obstacle Courses</h4>
          <p class="text-stone-600">Physical challenges on the estate: zip line, rope bridge, river crossing, log jumping, and more.</p>
       </div>
       <div>
          <img src="/images/campamento/CERAMICA.jpg" alt="Neolithic Pottery" class="w-full h-64 object-cover rounded-xl mb-4" />
          <h4 class="text-xl font-bold text-stone-800">Neolithic Pottery</h4>
          <p class="text-stone-600">We make Neolithic pots from clay using the same manufacturing, modeling, and decorating techniques as in the Neolithic period.</p>
       </div>       
       <div>
          <img src="/images/campamento/EXCURSIONES.jpeg" alt="Excursions and Bioblitz" class="w-full h-64 object-cover rounded-xl mb-4" />
          <h4 class="text-xl font-bold text-stone-800">Excursions and Bioblitz</h4>
          <p class="text-stone-600">Excursions to natural environments where we identify plant and animal species. We also learn to assess the water quality of our river using bioindicators.</p>
       </div>
